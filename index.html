Add Google Calendar

Instructions:

  1. On a computer, open Google Calendar.
  2. In the top right corner, click the Settings button and then Settings.
  3. On the left side of the screen, click the name of the calendar you want to add, which is located under "Settings for my calendars."
  4. In the "Integrate calendar" section, copy the "Public URL to this calendar."
  5. Paste the link in the text box below.
